Who We Are

Deep expertise.

Multi-Disciplinary Core

Our core members bring years of hands-on industry experience spanning visual design, local data storage, application performance optimization, privacy security technology, and interaction experience design. Each domain is represented at a deep expert level — not generalists, but specialists who collaborate.

Privacy-First Engineering

We employ a high-performance back-end architecture ensuring silky-smooth operation even under heavy data loads. We adhere to a serverless, account-free, tracking-free local development philosophy — user data is stored 100% in on-device encrypted databases. Users retain absolute control over their data, with full export freedom.

Design Innovation

We break the monotonous stereotype of utility apps by fusing unique visual aesthetics with interaction design. Every product features recognizable, admirable visual experiences — where minimalist functional logic meets exquisite design details.

Focused Product Domains

We focus on personal life-management tools — habit management, data tracking, content recording, and financial control. Complex functionality is translated into clean, intuitive workflows.

Continuous Innovation

Our team maintains an attitude of exploratory innovation, closely monitoring industry technology trends and evolving user needs. We continually iterate on our development philosophy and product forms.
