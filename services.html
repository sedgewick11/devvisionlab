How We Work

Our proven process.

01
Discovery & Requirements

Deep-dive into user needs, market positioning, technical constraints, and compliance requirements. We define the product's privacy architecture before a single line of code is written.

02
Design System & Prototyping

High-fidelity Figma prototypes with component libraries, motion guidelines, and accessibility specs. User testing before development begins.

03
Development & Privacy Engineering

Agile sprints with privacy-by-design at every layer. Code reviews, performance profiling, and security audits are baked in — not bolted on.

04
QA, Compliance & Submission

Comprehensive testing on real devices, App Store / Play Store compliance review, Privacy Label / Data Safety form completion, and staged rollout planning.

05
Post-Launch & Optimization

Analytics-driven iteration, ASO (App Store Optimization), ad mediation tuning, and continuous compliance monitoring against evolving global regulations.
