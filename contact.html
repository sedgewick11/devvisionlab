Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom app development for external clients?

Selectively, yes. We occasionally partner with businesses or individuals whose project aligns with our privacy-first philosophy and design standards. Reach out with details and we'll evaluate the fit.

How do I submit a GDPR data subject access request?

Email contact@devvisionlab.com with the subject "Data Subject Request" and we will respond within 7 business days. For users in the EU/UK, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

I found a bug in one of your apps. How do I report it?

Email support@devvisionlab.com with the app name, iOS/Android version, device model, and a description of the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings are extremely helpful.

Can I request a refund for an in-app purchase?

Refunds are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. Please initiate refund requests directly through those platforms. For assistance, contact support@devvisionlab.com and we'll provide guidance.

Are you open to press or media inquiries?

Absolutely. For press kits, founder interviews, or media features, contact us at contact@devvisionlab.com with "Media Inquiry" in the subject line.
